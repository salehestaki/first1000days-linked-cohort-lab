## 35–50 words

## 70–100 words

To demonstrate my readiness to work with linked longitudinal data before access to any restricted datasets, I developed a fully synthetic intergenerational cohort laboratory. It creates independent family, parent, birth, event, educational and outcome tables; constructs parental exposure windows across the first 1,000 days; audits family linkage and longitudinal integrity; compares conventional cohort and sibling fixed-effects designs against known simulation truth; and evaluates aggregate educational-trajectory models using family-grouped validation and calibration. The repository contains no real records, provides no individual risk ranking, and is strictly a research-methods demonstration.
