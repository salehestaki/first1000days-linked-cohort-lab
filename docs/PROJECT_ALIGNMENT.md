# Project alignment

This repository aligns methodologically with research on intergenerational mental health during the first 1,000 days by demonstrating linked multi-table architecture, parental timing windows, educational trajectories, sibling clusters, linkage-quality auditing, simulation-based causal-design comparisons, and family-safe predictive evaluation. It deliberately does not claim restricted-data access, empirical findings, clinical validity, real suicide prediction, governance readiness, or institutional endorsement.
